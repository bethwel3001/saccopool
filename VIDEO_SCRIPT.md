# SaccoChain — 2‑Minute Pitch Script

> Total runtime: 2:00. One presenter + screen share. All numbers below are illustrative —
> swap in real testnet figures before recording.

---

**[0:00 – 0:20] PROBLEM**

> "In Uganda, more than 5 million people rely on informal savings groups — SACCOs — to
> access credit. But these groups are plagued by mismanagement, lack of transparency, and
> zero access to outside capital. People lose their savings to a single bad treasurer and
> have no recourse."

*Visual:* a photo of a hand‑written SACCO ledger or treasurer cash box.

---

**[0:20 – 0:35] SOLUTION**

> "SaccoChain puts the SACCO on‑chain. Deposits, loans and repayments are all handled by
> a smart contract on Stellar — transparent, automatic, and accessible from any
> smartphone. We forked Blend Protocol — Stellar's lending primitive — and tuned it for
> micro‑lending: USDC only, a 2× borrow cap, and a one‑glance member dashboard."

*Visual:* SaccoChain landing page.

---

**[0:35 – 1:30] LIVE DEMO** *(screen share)*

> "Here I connect my Freighter wallet…"
> *Click Connect Wallet, approve in Freighter.*
>
> "The dashboard reads straight from the SaccoPool contract — total pool size, number of
> members, current APR."
>
> "I deposit **10 USDC** into the pool…"
> *Click Deposit, sign in Freighter.*
> "Here's the transaction on Stellar Expert — final in five seconds, fee under a tenth of a
> cent."
>
> "The contract's `get_member_stats` view now reports I'm allowed to borrow up to
> **20 USDC** — twice my deposit — exactly the SACCO rule, but enforced by code instead of
> a treasurer."
>
> "I borrow **15 USDC**…"
> *Click Borrow, sign, show tx hash.*
>
> "And any other member — anywhere in the world — can see the same pool balance and the
> same activity in real time. There is no off‑chain database. There is no treasurer's
> notebook. There is only the contract."

---

**[1:30 – 1:50] WHO IT'S FOR & GROWTH PATH**

> "We're targeting SACCO treasurers and members in Uganda first. A 50‑member SACCO can go
> fully on‑chain in a single afternoon. From there we approach the Uganda Cooperative
> Alliance — 6,000+ registered SACCOs nationally — to onboard whole districts at a time."

---

**[1:50 – 2:00] CLOSE**

> "SaccoChain — transparent savings, trustless lending, built for Africa on Stellar."

*Visual:* logo + live URL + GitHub URL on a clean end card.
